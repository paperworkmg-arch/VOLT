# Product Management

## PRD Template
- Problem statement (1 paragraph)
- Target user (ICP)
- Solution overview
- Success metrics (North Star + supporting)
- User stories with acceptance criteria
- Out of scope (explicit)

## Market Analysis
- TAM/SAM/SOM calculation
- Competitive landscape (feature matrix)
- Differentiation (why us?)
- Pricing strategy

## Roadmap
- Now (this sprint)
- Next (next sprint)
- Later (backlog)
- Priority: impact vs effort matrix
