Title: “Last Year Is Still All Over Me”: Soft Loft Reckon With Old Ghosts, Bad Timing, and the Gamble of Growing Up Through Restless, Radiant Indie Pop
Message: "Swiss five-piece Soft Loft chase the ghosts of old love through “F U WANT IT,” a spectacular indie pop anthem from their forthcoming sophomore album ‘Throw the Dice’ that captures the rush, panic, and strange freedom of growing up unfinished. In conversation with Atwood Magazine, frontwoman Jorina Stamm opens up about the record’s pull between rebellion and reason, the late-twenties extremes of craving calm while fearing you’re not living wildly enough, and the collective chemistry helping Soft Loft grow into their boldest, fullest sound yet."
