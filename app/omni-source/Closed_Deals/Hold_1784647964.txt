Title: “Hold Me Like You Used to Do”: Luca Wilding Bathes Maternal Love in Boundless Light on “Ocean Mother,” a Soul-Stirring Dream-Folk Reverie
Message: "British dream-folk singer/songwriter Luca Wilding delivers one of the most breathtaking works of his career with “Ocean Mother,” a luminous, majestic song that lifts the bond between mother and child into a vast inheritance held in memory and the sea. Shot in Zanzibar, filmmaker Steve Won’s beautiful, deeply human music video gives that devotion a living pulse, following three generations of women whose linked hands, sunlit rituals, and shared laughter carry that love from one life into the next."
