Title: “It Seems Too Personal When We’re Not Friends”: Brooke Annibale’s Achingly Sweet “Birthday” Captures the Awkward Afterlife of Friendship
Message: "Indie folk singer/songwriter Brooke Annibale holds affection and distance in the same tender frame on “Birthday,” a gently brooding reflection on the yearly landmark that keeps a once-close bond alive in memory long after the friendship itself has faded. In conversation with Atwood Magazine, Annibale opens up about how that emotional precision carries into 'Bolder Font,' the self-produced album that helped her rebuild her confidence and reconnect with the joy of making music."
