Title: “The Music Is the Softest Part of Me”: Fenne Lily on Saying “Uh Huh” to Love – Even When It Hurts
Message: "Singer/songwriter Fenne Lily lets old pain and new love coexist on “Uh Huh,” the warm, weathered, and wistful lead single from her long-awaited fourth studio album, ‘Win Win.’ In conversation with Atwood Magazine, she shares lessons from her first decade in music, celebrates the friends who gave her new record its communal pulse, and reveals why this shrug-and-a-wink song had to lead her next chapter."
