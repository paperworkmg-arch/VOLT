Title: “I Hate Myself, So I Turn It Into Art”: Medium Build Rips Through Every Defense Mechanism on “Armor,” a Feverish Reckoning with the Terror of Being Known
Message: "Medium Build’s Nick Carpenter barrels through intrusive thoughts and lifelong defense mechanisms on “Armor,” an exhilarating rush of panic and dark humor that transforms self-interrogation into a communal, cathartic, and all-consuming release. As the first glimpse of his sixth studio album ‘King of Having Fun,’ “Armor” sets the terms for a record that scrapes joy from the wreckage without pretending the wound has healed."
