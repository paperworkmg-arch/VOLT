Title: Let’s Create Again: Joe Strummer, the Rare Rock Star Who Didn’t Seem Changed by Fame
Message: "Joe Strummer spent years fronting “The Only Band That Matters,” but this examination is less interested in The Clash’s peak than in what happened afterward. Moving through his history and the quietly inspiring documentary ‘Let’s Rock Again!,’ the piece explores why Strummer’s greatest legacy may have been his ability to remain present, curious, and creatively alive long after most rock stars begin chasing their own tails."
