Title: “Falling in Love With the Chaos”: GIMMY’s Gemma Owens on Refusing to Be Molded by the Music Industry and Their Latest ‘Labour of Love’
Message: "Australian indie surf/garage band GIMMY make eccentric, emotional music with no boundaries, pairing unapologetically honest, witty songwriting with a sound built to be played loud. In conversation with Atwood Magazine, frontwoman Gemma Owens dives into the emotional chaos behind her band’s raucous sophomore EP ‘Labour of Love’ while reflecting on creative burnout and rediscovering the enthusiasm to build a career on her own terms."
