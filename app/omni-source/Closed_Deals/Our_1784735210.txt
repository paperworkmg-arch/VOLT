Title: Our Take: Gracie Abrams Alchemizes Her Pain into a Cathartic Symphony on ‘Daughter from Hell’
Message: "Dark, gritty, and deeply vulnerable, Gracie Abrams’s third album ‘Daughter from Hell’ finds the singer/songwriter collaborating with producer and co-writer Aaron Dessner to pair her most biting lyrics and dynamic vocals yet with sweeping orchestral arrangements, transforming heartbreak, anxiety, and self-doubt into the boldest, most musically expansive work of her career – and a striking declaration of her artistic maturity."
